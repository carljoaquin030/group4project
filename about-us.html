Who We Are


Look and Hoop Creations was officially established in March 2026 by five dedicated individuals: Ronald Allan SP. Bularin, Ram Justine S. Dalman, John Randel L. Olinares, Yesha Mae P. Convento, and Carl Joaquin Tiburcio. The business was founded with shared vision of promoting crochet as both a creative art and a sustainable source of income.


Inspired by the rich cultural background of crochet, the founders aimed to create unique, handmade products while building a community that values craftmanship and creativity. Since its establishment, Look and Hoop Creations continues to combine traditional crochet techniques with modern designs to meet the needs and taste of its customers.


To create high-quality, unique crochet products while empowering artisans, practicing sustainability, and delighting our customers.


To craft timeless crochet creations that inspire creativity and connect communities worldwide.
